Community Guidelines

Together We Help Each Other

QC Help Support is a safe space for Quezon City residents to seek and offer help during emergencies and daily needs.

Community Rules
1
Be Respectful

Treat all community members with kindness. No bullying, harassment, or hate speech.

2
Post Truthfully

Only post verified information. False reports during emergencies can cause panic and harm.

3
No Spam

Do not post the same request multiple times. Update your existing post instead.

4
Protect Privacy

Do not share personal details publicly. Use messages for sensitive information.

5
Tag Correctly

Use the right tags so responders can find urgent requests quickly.

🆘 How to Request Help
1
Tap the + button or "What's happening in your barangay?"
2
Choose the right tag — Help Needed, Flood, Medical, etc.
3
Add your location (barangay and street if safe to share)
4
Set urgency level — High / Medium / Low
5
Add a photo if it helps describe the situation
6
Update the status once help has arrived or the issue is resolved
Safety Guidelines
Life-threatening emergency?

Call 911 first, then post on QC Help.

Never share passwords

QC Help staff will never ask for your password.

Report abuse

Use the flag icon to report fake or inappropriate posts.

Mental health

Call 1-800-1888-1999 for 24/7 mental health support.

By using QC Help Support, you agree to follow these community guidelines.

Violations may result in account suspension.

Our Purpose

QC Help Support connects Quezon City residents during emergencies, natural disasters, and everyday community needs. We believe that neighbors helping neighbors creates a stronger, safer community for everyone.

How to Request Help

  • Create a free account with your email
  • Tap the + button to create a post
  • Choose the right tag (Flood, Medical, Missing, etc.)
  • Add your exact location and a photo if safe to do so
  • Set the urgency level — High, Medium, or Low
  • Submit and the community will respond

How to Volunteer

  • Browse help requests on the Home Feed
  • Tap Offer Help on any post
  • Choose how you can assist (supplies, transport, medical, etc.)
  • Add a personal message to coordinate directly
  • Mark posts as "Resolved" once help is delivered

Reputation System

Your community reputation grows as you help others. Earn points for every post (+10) and every help you give (+25).
